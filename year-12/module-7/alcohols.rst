Fermentation
^^^^^^^^^^^^
Fermentation is a process which utilises yeast or other enzymes to break down glucose (:math:`\mathrm{C}_{6}\mathrm{H}_{12}\mathrm{O}_{6}`)molecules are broken down into ethanol (:math:`\mathrm{C}_2\mathrm{H}_5\mathrm{OH}`) and carbon dioxide (:math:`\mathrm{CO}_2`)
The reaction is as follows:
    :math:`\mathrm{C}_{6}\mathrm{H}_{12}\mathrm{O}_{6} => \mathrm{C}_2\mathrm{H}_5\mathrm{OH} + \mathrm{CO}_2`
